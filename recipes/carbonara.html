Pasta alla carbonara

Description

You know what carbonara is. I'm not gonna describe it.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Pecorino
  • Salt (the big one)
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the pasta
  2. Slice the salt into cubes
  3. Throw the eggs in the oven
  4. Enjoy!
